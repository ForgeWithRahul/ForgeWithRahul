Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Definition

AI is the field of computer science that creates systems capable of performing tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Why AI Exists

AI automates complex tasks, reduces human error, and allows solutions beyond manual human capability.

Examples

  • Search engines & recommendation systems
  • Face recognition & smart cameras
  • Voice assistants & NLP tools
  • Robotics & autonomous systems
  • Predictive analytics & self-learning algorithms
